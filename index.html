About

Badr AZAOU, I was born on 22/12/2002 in Marrakesh. I grew up in Chichoua and has always been interested in Developping my actual situation and making life easier by getting things donr in better ways. I attended High School and later went on to study Computer science at University Cadi Ayad (Faculty Of Sciences Semlalia).

As a computer science student, I have developed a strong foundation in programming languages like Javascript, PHP, Java and C++. I am skilled in database management and web development. I am passionate about learning and staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in the field. Through personal coding projects and participation in coding programs, I have gained practical experience in developing software solutions to real-world problems.
